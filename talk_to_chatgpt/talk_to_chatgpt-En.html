Example 2 – Talk with ChatGPT

How to practice daily conversation with ChatGPT You can say to ChatGPT: - I want us to talk in German. - Please teach me very simple sentences I can use with neighbors or friends. - After every German sentence, write the English translation in brackets. - If I make a mistake, please correct me gently and show the right sentence. ChatGPT might start like this: ChatGPT: Hallo! Wie geht es dir? (Hello! How are you?) You: Mir geht es gut, danke. Und dir? (I’m fine, thanks. And you?) Useful lines to practice: - Wo wohnst du? (Where do you live?) - Ich wohne in Berlin. (I live in Berlin.) - Schönes Wetter heute! (Nice weather today!) Tip: Practice 10 minutes daily and ask ChatGPT to quiz you at the end.
